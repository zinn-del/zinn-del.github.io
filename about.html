AN EPISTLE FROM OUR FOUNDER

I started this amazing line of cosmetics in 2005 when i noticed their was no organic product for natural hair care. I decided to create various formulations to enable Nigerians in her locality to have access to affordable and natural hair product. Over the years, we have since gain a strong foothold in the African markets. We know we are not where we want to be as the leading and affordable cosmetics product manufacturer in Africa. I consider us to be a strong force in the market and we pledge to continue producing affordable products with strong consideration for our eco-system.
